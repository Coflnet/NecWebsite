## Boring routes are often durable routes

The appeal of NPC-to-bazaar flips is not that they create huge per-trade margins. The appeal is that the logic is clean. If players still pay the bazaar price for an item obtainable at an NPC, and daily caps or inconvenience keep the route from being farmed to zero, the flip can stay alive much longer than flashy content-driven strategies. That is exactly why these routes deserve a place in a guide library aimed at long-term search traffic: new players underestimate them and advanced players often skip writing about them.

The best way to approach the method is as a discipline test:

- Can you accept smaller but cleaner wins?
- Can you check caps, transport time and exit liquidity without getting bored?

Players who can do that often end up with steadier income than players constantly jumping to the newest screenshot-friendly margin.
