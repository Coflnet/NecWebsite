## The biggest confirmed changes

The official thread introduced 25 new Abiphone contacts and expanded Abiphone trio capacity, which matters more than it first sounds because it changes how many everyday NPC conveniences players can keep active at once. The same patch added Littlefoot's Den in the Glacite Mineshafts, along with new Littlefoot rewards including the Littlefoot Shard and Littlefoot's Fluff for the Baby Yeti mythic upgrade path. Fishing also got broad support: Jerry Island was refreshed, hotspots were added, winter fishing behavior became clearer and several item and stat systems were cleaned up.

Farming quietly got some of the most durable QoL. The greenhouse systems became easier to read through mutation previews, fully grown crop effects stayed locked until ready and the Cultivation requirement was reduced. The Reforge Anvil was also discontinued, which is the kind of systems cleanup that does not dominate thumbnails but absolutely changes player expectations.

| Farming | Mutation previews, locked crop effects until ready, lower Cultivation requirement | Greenhouse systems become easier to read |
| Reforging | Reforge Anvil discontinued | Systems cleanup that resets player expectations |

## Best day-one checklist

If you are logging in after the patch, the clean route is straightforward. Update your Abiphone contacts, test the fishing outpost changes, check whether you can unlock or expand greenhouse progress, and only then decide whether any market move is worth chasing. If you run Glacite Mineshafts, add Littlefoot to your watchlist, but treat those drops like a measured hunt rather than free money.
