## What players are really searching for

Players rarely search for tool comparison alone. They search for phrases like AH scanner, flip finder, sold data, historical sale context, modifier-aware pricing, focused filtering, fire-sale alerts, real-time bazaar prices, craft engine and profit simulator. Those phrases matter because they describe different jobs, not one generic tool.

That is why workflow fit matters more than surface-level feature counts. If you need fast auction references, your best tool may be different from the one you would choose for passive bazaar depth or craft discovery.

## Which tool should you actually use?

If you want the widest live stack, choose SkyCofl. If you want ranked discovery boards, use Skyblock.bz. If you think in bazaar depth, demand and repeatable order flow, BazaarTracker is the cleanest option. If you want a premium all-in-one dashboard that bundles those tasks into one interface, BazaarPro makes the most sense.
