## Pest farming begins with controlled conditions

The most useful advice in the current pest-farming material is structural: the route depends on building for pest generation and cleanup, not simply harvesting the crop you like most. That means plot decisions matter, traffic through the farm matters and gear choices matter in a way that basic early Garden play does not. Players who jump in without restructuring often end up with a setup that is technically pest-capable but not actually efficient enough to justify the attention.

This is why specialized equipment like Helenthis armor gets so much discussion. Pest farming pays players who lean into the dedicated loop. If you half-build the method, you often inherit the setup cost without getting the corresponding consistency. That is a recurring pattern across SkyBlock money methods: specialization only shines once the specialization is real.

## Do not confuse showcase numbers with baseline results

Pest farming content often includes flashy money numbers, but the stable takeaway is more conservative. The route becomes strong when your Garden foundation is already solid and you are ready to commit to the branch.
